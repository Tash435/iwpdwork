About School of Business Administration (SOBA)

The purpose of this site is to provide information about The School of Business Administration (SOBA), the leading instituion for undergraduate business education in the Caribbean. School of Business Administration is located at the University of Technology, Jamaica, Papine Campus, Building #5, 237 Old Hope Road, Kgn. 6 and Western Campus, Dome Street, Montego Bay, James. At SOBA, we offer both the Bachelor of Business Administration and Bachelor of Science degrees. Our degree programs are structured to provide graduates with the skills and information needed to significantly advance both the private and public sectors. Passes in five (5) CSEC/GCE O-Level topics, including English language and mathematics, are required for admission to our programs.
