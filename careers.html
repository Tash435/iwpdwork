Career Opportunities in the School of Business Administration

Explore some of the interesting career paths that are accessible to UTech School of Business Administration graduates. Each major has a list of possible job opportunities that you can pursue after completing your degree!

Marketing Major

Description: Oversees marketing campaigns, branding strategies, and market research to drive customer engagement and business growth.

Career Opportunities: Brand Manager, Product Manager, Marketing Research Analyst, PR specialist, Account executive

Finance Major

Description: Analyzes financial data to help organizations make informed investment decisions and improve financial performance.

Careers Opportunities: Financial Advisor, Loan Manager/Officer, Pension Fund Administrator, Securities Analyst, Stock Trader

Human Resource Major

Description: Manages recruitment, training, and employee relations within an organization.

Career Opportunities:HR Manager, Recruitment Manager, Compensation & Benefits Manager, Labour Relations manager

Productions & Operations Management Major

Description: A Production and Operations Manager is responsible for overseeing all aspects of a company's production process

Career Opportunities: Logistics Manager, Production Planner, Supply Chain Manager, Project Manager, Operation Analyst
